12 May 2021

Celotajs updated:

  • Added a configuration option for splitting the admin tab into multiple tabs
  • Interviews have been moved to the Tickets tab
  • It is now possible for administrators to split the admin tab into multiple tabs

Cocacola4124 updated:

  • Fixes exploit in orion arcade machine that allowed players to steal, sell, and buy resources without being at a spaceport.

Fikou updated:

  • icebox no longer has more prisoner slots
  • because of this, you can no longer latejoin as prisoner

Ghilker updated:

  • firelocks won't close if there is an atmos holofan on their turf.

Guillaume Prata updated:

  • After many issues with plasmaman prisoners escaping perma and hiding in maint, NT will supply the station with up to date envirosuits with their suit sensors locked, just like other prisoner uniforms.

JJRcop updated:

  • Maintenance Drones are once again constructable in Robotics.
  • The Drone Dispenser has not made a return.
  • New settings DRONE_REQUIRED_ROLE, and DRONE_ROLE_PLAYTIME, which determine the playtime exp required for playing drones.

JohnFulpWillard updated:

  • Command personnel can now use the Science Hub tablet app they start with.

Ryll/Shaps updated:

  • Added keybinds for moving up/down z-levels, useful as a ghost on multi-z maps! These default to the page up/page down buttons (previously used as the secondary binds for switching hands and activating items in hand).
  • Examining an AI's camera eye as a ghost will now show you their laws, the same as if you examined their core

actioninja updated:

  • radials layer correctly

jlsnow301 updated:

  • The cryopod machine has been added, an IC way to reopen job slots for yourself and other players.
  • AFK and SSD players can be placed into cryopods to reopen their job slot.
  • Refactored cult sacrifice targeting to compensate for players logging out.

necromanceranne updated:

  • Monkeys can no longer get into mechs.

norill updated:

  • fixed infinite airlock electronics from deconstructing blast doors

tralezab updated:

  • honorbound god won't get mad anymore for JUSTICE.
  • captains name their respective celestial bodies
  • added podspawning helper codewise, most code pod spawns use it

13 April 2021

ArcaneDefence updated:

  • You can now choose to have a fear of guns if you take the phobia quirk.

BootlegBow updated:

  • fixed grammar on computer hardware tech node

FernandoJ8 updated:

  • Atmospherics Envirogloves are no longer insulated.

Ghilker updated:

  • fix HFR corners disappearing on build
  • color adapters can connect again to all colors regardless
  • Allow the pneumatic cannon to be created with the smart pipes fitting
  • fix deltastation and icebox toxins from merging some pipes with distro/waste

Greniza updated:

  • added non-adjustable mask which allows internals, is sterile, and muzzles a patient or victim, complete with sprites
  • replaced standard sterile breathmask in anesthetic closet with above muzzle breathmask
  • adds BLOCKS_SPEECH clothing flag and reworked muzzle code and is_muzzled() function be based on flag

IndieanaJones updated:

  • Space Dragon's gust attack is now tied to right click instead of an ability button
  • Space Dragon has received numerous balance changes, most notably that Space Dragon will stall the shuttle until it loses or completes its objectives
  • Fixed numerous bugs with Space Dragon, most notably the rifts not vanishing after Space Dragon's death
  • Space Dragon now has a custom roar for dying and winning
  • Deleted now unused Space Dragon ability icon
  • Space Dragon's speed changes are now controlled using movespeed modifiers instead of direct speed adjustments

JoshAdamPowell updated:

  • skillchip implanters are now more efficient!

Kangarad updated:

  • Ashwalkers have been seen to not be affected by viruses anymore, likely due to evolution

LemonInTheDark updated:

  • Some stuff that applied blurred vision and roatated things on screen wasn't working, let's fix that up shall we?
  • Rotting things are less potent, and will ramp up their effectiveness over time, rather then being all or nothing
  • Radiation related pluoxium will now only generate in gasmixes under 200K, remember to keep that sm cold

Melbert updated:

  • Slippery things in your pocket no longer make you slippery. If you want to become slippery, you need to wear slippery things on your person actual.

Mothblocks updated:

  • Fixed humans not having any eyes.

SgtHunk updated:

  • Fixed a small typo that existed on the medical kiosk's radiation poisoning warning.

nightred updated:

  • Skeletons heal more when drinking milk
  • fixed reagents not metabolizing properly
